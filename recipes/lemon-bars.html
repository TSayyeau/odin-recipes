Lemon Protein Bars

Description

Clean eating doesn't have to be boring. It can be delicious and good for you. This recipe proves just that with it's sweet and tangy goodness. Dessert has never been so good. This is great for after the gym or just when you are craving something sweet.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Oat Flour
  • 2 scoops Protein Powder, vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 5 packets Crystal Light, lemon
  • 4 Large Egg Whites
  • 3 serving packets Splenda
  • 1 cup Apple Sauce, unsweetened
  • 4 fl. oz. Water

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Mix oat flour, protein powder, salt, baking soda, and crystal light in a large bowl.
  3. Mix egg whites, splenda, apple sauce, and water in a separate bowl.
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix together.
  5. Spray an 8x8 glass pyrex dish with non-stick spray.
  6. Pour mixture into dish and bake 23 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool and cut into squares.
